The NFL announced the following scenarios for the Broncos (vs. Los Angeles Chargers, Sunday, Jan. 4, 2:25 p.m., CBS) to clinch the AFC's No. 1 seed in Week 18:
Denver clinches the AFC's No. 1 seed, lone first-round bye and home-field advantage with:
1. DEN win OR
2. DEN tie + NE loss or tie OR
3. NE loss + JAX loss or tie
